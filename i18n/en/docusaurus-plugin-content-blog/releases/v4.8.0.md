LAND IT version 4.8.0 is now available. Below is the list of changes and new features.

<!-- truncate -->

-    Creation of an abstraction mechanism to support criteria for creating new UIs (such as the AZR Strips example).
-    Adaptation of several functionalities to support the new extended attributes.
-    Implementation of simultaneous editing of multiple polygons in the POSP.
-    Possibility to copy several polygons at the same time, both from user layers and from the POSA to the POSP.
-    Modification of the operation sequences menu to display 5 operations at a time, instead of 3.
-    Correction of bugs associated with fence calculation.
-    Corrections and improvements to functional tests.

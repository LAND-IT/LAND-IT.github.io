LAND IT version 4.4.0 is now available. Below is the list of changes and new features.

<!-- truncate -->

-    Addition of four economic models developed by SBE students - maritime pine, eucalyptus, olive groves and cork oak (available soon).
-    Addition of natural breaks (jenks) mode in graduated style editing.
-    Queries are working again.
-    Help button for geoprocessing tools available again.
-    Addition of geometric tools to user layers:
    -   Union, split and buffer applied to lines and polygons.
    -   Clip and identity applied to polygons.
-    Addition of a topological validation mechanism:
    -   Identification of intersections between polygons.
    -   Identification of gaps between polygons.
-    Correction of the interactive legend.
-    Corrections in the calculation of ecosystem services.
-    Corrections when exporting a version.
-    Corrections in the split geometric tool.

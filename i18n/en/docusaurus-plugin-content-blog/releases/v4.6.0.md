LAND IT version 4.6.0 is now available. Below is the list of changes and new features.

<!-- truncate -->

-    Various corrections and improvements in the processes of importing and exporting scenario versions.
-    Correction of bugs associated with Geometric Tools.
-    Correction of bugs associated with Filters.
-    Implementation of the filter sharing mechanism.
     -   Allows users to publish their filters and import public filters shared by other users.
-    Integration of filters in version statistics.

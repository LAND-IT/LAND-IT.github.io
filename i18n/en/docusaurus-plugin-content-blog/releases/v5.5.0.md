Version 5.5.0 of LAND IT is now available. Below is the list of changes and new features.

<!-- truncate -->

- Modifications to the Map movement mechanism. By default, the tool now assumes the shape of a "hand", aligning with the practices of other GIS.
- Implementation of a new selection tool that enables the visualization of information associated with geometries.
- Development of tutorials in video format.
- Development of accessibility methods for selection tools, including the use of keys such as the ESC key to cancel selected tools.
- Addition of more translation entries for English
- Fixes for various bugs
